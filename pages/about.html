About I2KNA

Internet 2000s Nostalgia Archive (I2KNA) is an archive project focused on preserving the atmosphere, designs, and culture of the 2000s internet era.

What is I2KNA?

This website collects information about websites, software, wallpapers, and other digital experiences from the early internet era.

The goal is to recreate the feeling of the Y2K period through archives, resources, and original creations.
